Why e-commerce?

In our highly connected world, more and more shopping is being done online. Loyal customers are hungry to support local businesses, while others are just curious to see what's new, but to reach both, a strong online presence is necessary. It's a no-brainer: There's only upside to selling to a wider market, especially if you don't have to physically be present or pay rent on brick and mortar. Not only that, your best customers find your store and come directly to you!
